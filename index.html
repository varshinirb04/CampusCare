What makes it different

A complaint system that feels alive, not like a form dump.

01

Smart intake

Students submit category, location, priority, description, and proof so the right team gets clear context from the start.

02

Status timeline

Every complaint moves through Pending, Assigned, In Progress, Resolved, and Feedback with visible ownership.

03

Feedback loop

After resolution, students can confirm whether the issue was solved so departments can improve service quality.

Workflow

From complaint to resolution in four visible steps.

The interface is designed for students, admins, and service teams to know exactly what happened, who owns it, and what comes next.

1

Register and verify student profile

Keeps complaints authentic and linked to a reachable campus user.

2

Raise an issue with exact location

Teams can filter by block, room, category, and urgency.

3

Track progress on the ticket board

No repeated calls or confusion about whether the complaint was seen.

4

Close with rating and feedback

Resolution quality becomes visible and measurable.
